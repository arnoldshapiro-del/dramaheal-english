What is PTSD?

PTSD â€” Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder â€” happens when your brain gets stuck in 'danger mode' after something really scary or harmful. It's not your fault, and it doesn't mean you're broken. Trauma Types Explained

Trauma is not one-size-fits-all. Understanding the type of trauma you experienced can help you and your therapist find the best approach.

Acute Trauma (single event)

Acute trauma results from a single overwhelming event â€” an accident, an assault, a natural disaster, witnessing violence. It happens once, but the impact can last.
